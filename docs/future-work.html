Future Work

Taleem is intentionally conservative about future plans.

This page lists directions, not promises.

More Slide Templates

Additional slide templates may be added over time.

Each new template must:

  • solve a real explanation problem
  • remain stable once introduced
  • not affect existing content

Author-Defined Templates

In the future, it may be possible for advanced users or systems to define custom slide templates.

This would extend the system without weakening its core principles.

Improved Tooling

Supporting tools may be added, such as validators, generators, or preview utilities.

These tools will not change the underlying model.

More Engines, Not More Features

Future expansion is more likely to happen through new engines than through new slide behavior.

This preserves content portability.

What Will Not Change

The core belief will remain:

Slides are selected, not edited.

Any future work must align with this.
