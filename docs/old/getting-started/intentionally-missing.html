What Is Intentionally Missing

Taleem deliberately excludes several features commonly found in presentation systems.

These omissions are not technical limitations. They are design decisions.

  • Timeline-based playback engines
  • Complex animations and transitions
  • Visual slide editors
  • Advanced equation and pointer slides

These features may be added later, but only after real teaching experience justifies them.

Stability and clarity take precedence over feature count.
